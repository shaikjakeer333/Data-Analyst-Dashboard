# Maven Analytics(Interactive Dashboard creation using Power-BI)
## Project Objective
Maven Analytics assigned the task of creating an Annual Executive Sales & Supply Chain Report for Wonka's Sweet using Power BI. The objective was to design an interactive dashboard that provides stakeholders with comprehensive insights into sales performance, profitability, target achievement, product performance, and supply chain operations to support strategic business decisions.

# Questions (KPIs)
- How many total orders were placed during the selected period?
- What is the total sales revenue generated?
- How much total profit was earned?
- What is the overall profit margin (%)?
- What is the annual sales target?
- What percentage of the sales target has been achieved?
- How many total units were sold?

## Projects Insights
📈 Total Sales reached 28.95K, achieving 64% of the annual target while generating 18.94K in total profit.
🎯 Chocolate Division is the highest revenue contributor, accounting for over 90% of total sales.
🏭 Lot's O' Nuts Factory emerged as the top-performing factory in both sales and profitability.
📍 California recorded the highest sales, making it the leading revenue-generating state.
📊 Sales showed a strong upward trend in Q4, with November and December recording the highest monthly sales.
🍫 Wonka Bar – Milk Chocolate is the best-selling product, followed closely by Wonka Bar – Scrumdidlyumptious.
💰 Despite strong sales, the business has achieved only 64% of its annual target, indicating room for growth.
📦 The business processed approximately 2K orders and sold 8K units during the selected period.
📉 The Other and Sugar divisions contributed a relatively small share of revenue, highlighting opportunities for performance improvement.
🚀 The dashboard identifies top-performing products, divisions, factories, and regions, enabling data-driven strategic decision-making.

## Final Conclusion 
The Executive Sales & Supply Chain Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by consolidating key sales, profit, target, product, factory, and regional metrics into a single interactive report. The analysis shows that the Chocolate Division is the primary revenue driver, Lot's O' Nuts Factory delivers the strongest operational performance, and California leads in sales. Although the business has achieved 64% of its annual sales target, the strong sales growth in Q4 indicates positive momentum. This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and make informed, data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance and achieve future targets
